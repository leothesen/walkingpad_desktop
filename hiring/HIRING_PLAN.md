# Hiring Plan — WalkingPad Desktop

## Overview
This document outlines the hiring strategy for the WalkingPad Desktop team to scale from a prototype to a polished product.

## Current Team
- **CEO**: Strategy, prioritization, board communication.
- **CTO**: Technical roadmap, architecture, engineering leadership.
- **UX Designer**: User research, design system, product UX.
- **Founding Engineer**: Core implementation, BLE, macOS development.

## Phase 2: Core Build (Current)
We have successfully hired our Founding Engineer. The focus is now on execution.

### Next Hires (Target: Q2 2026)
1. **CMO (Chief Marketing Officer)**
   - **Role**: Own growth, content, and community.
   - **Priority**: Medium (Start search late Q2).
   - **Focus**: Prepare for public launch, manage social presence, and build the "WalkingPad Community".

## Hiring Process
1. **Source**: Internal referrals and targeted outreach in the macOS/Swift developer community.
2. **Interview**:
   - CEO: Culture fit and vision alignment.
   - CTO/Designer: Technical/Design skill assessment.
   - Team: Peer interview for collaboration check.
3. **Offer**: Competitive packages with equity for early hires.

## Onboarding
- **Week 1**: Codebase deep-dive, environment setup, and small "quick win" tasks.
- **Week 2**: Full ownership of a sub-feature or module.
- **Month 1**: Contribution to core architectural decisions.
